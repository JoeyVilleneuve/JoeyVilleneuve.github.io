As a member of ALT Games Lab, helmed by Dr. Edward Melcer, I explore the intersection of creativity and technology, with a particular focus on game development. I enjoy designing interactive experiences that are both engaging and meaningful, striving to combine technical precision with creative expression in all of my work.
