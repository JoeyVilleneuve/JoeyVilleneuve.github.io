Building for both sides of the screen.

I'm a Computer Science and Human-Computer Interaction graduate from Carleton University. HCI taught me to design for the person in front of the screen, while CS taught me to architect what runs behind it. I believe great software requires both: an exceptional experience for the user, and elegant engineering behind the scenes. I focus primarily on games, but I apply that same intentionality to my work with tools, data, and interactive applications.
